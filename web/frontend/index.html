About this app

This application transforms your photos into stunning works of art inspired by the unique style of Vincent Van Gogh, using the power of artificial intelligence. Our model has been trained to understand and reproduce the master’s characteristic brushstrokes, vibrant colors, and expressive textures, creatively applying them to your images. Developed with passion by lovers of both art and technology, this app aims to inspire you to see everyday moments through a new lens—one filled with colors, strokes, and emotions reminiscent of Van Gogh’s masterpieces. At the core of this app is a Pix2Pix convolutional neural network, a type of deep learning model designed for image-to-image translation. Pix2Pix works by learning how to map an input image to a corresponding target image, enabling the model to "paint" your photos in a specific style.

For training, we used a dataset of Van Gogh’s paintings and drawings obtained from WikiArt as the target images. The input images were contour representations of these artworks, generated using techniques such as K-means clustering (to simplify colors) and median blur (to smooth the shapes). By training on pairs of input and target images, the model learns to fill in the contours with colors and textures that replicate Van Gogh’s style. When you create a new image, the app first converts your photo into a contour-like version using the same K-means and median blur process. This processed image is then passed through the trained Pix2Pix generator, which produces a final image in Van Gogh’s distinctive artistic style.
